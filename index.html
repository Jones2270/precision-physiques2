About

Who am I

My name is Jonathan Clarke (Johnny) I am 43 Years old and live in Caerphilly, South Wales.

I have been in the Fitness Industry for over 20 years.


I am a fully qualified Personal Trainer, Gym Instructor and Weight Management Consultant.

Myself and wife Claire opened a personal training studio in May of 2023. I currently have over 80 PT Clients on the books.

Within the first 7 months of opening, I conducted more than 1000 PT sessions (1002 to be exact)


Awards

Awarded 2nd place for Welsh Gym Of The Year 2023 in the National Fitness Awards, I also placed 3rd for Fitness Entrepreneur 2023 in the same awards.


Motivation

My love for health and fitness started when I was young. Today, I’ve been fortunate to turn my passion into a thriving profession. Since 2019 I’ve been working as a qualified trainer in south Wales, guiding my clients towards a healthier lifestyle.


Through working with a diverse group of clients, I’ve come to realize that everybody is unique, each with their own goals and lifestyle. I will construct a plan and training schedule according to your fitness experience and needs that will have you seeing results in no time.
